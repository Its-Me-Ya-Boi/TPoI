Trans Peers of Illinois

Trans Peers of Illinois (TPoI) is a social group for everyone on the gender spectrum. We convene every Friday from 6:45 PM to 9 PM!

RULES

  1. Be respectful. We do not allow transphobia, homophobia, sexism, racism, or any other forms of prejudice.
  2. No harassment, bullying, aggressive behavior, or stalking.
  3. Avoid express venting, especially that which is warrent of medical care. Co-facilitators are not trained counselors and are not trained for any crisises.
  4. Partners not on the gender spectrum are welcome as long as they follow the rules and understand this group is for those on the gender spectrum.
  5. Co-facilitators have the right to remove anyone that disobeys the rules or causes an overtly uncomfortable environment.
